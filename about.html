About Jo Gifts

Our Story

Jo Gifts began with a simple goal — to turn everyday gifts into extraordinary keepsakes. Our passion lies in creativity, color, and customization. Whether it’s a vinyl-printed shirt, a personalized mug, or a heartfelt hamper, every item we make is designed with love and attention to detail.

What We Offer

We specialize in:

  • Custom clothing & apparel
  • Gift sets and hampers
  • Personalized glassware and keychains
  • Vinyl and sublimation prints

Why Choose Us?

We don’t just make gifts — we create moments. Each item is made to match your vibe, your event, and your story. From “just because” surprises to milestone celebrations, we’re here to help you make memories that last.
